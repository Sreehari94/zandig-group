About Us

ZANDIG KNOWLEDGE MANAGEMENT Pvt. Ltd. was conceptualized on 1st October 1998 at Bangalore by a group of professionals from Industry. They have over 20 years of industrial experience and 16 years of experience in implementation of Quality Management system in various industries. Over a period of nearly a decade the Zandig have undertaken successfully comprehensive turnkey projects for implementation of Quality Management System and help obtain ISO certificates for over 150 organizations ranging from Small Scale Industries to Multinationals, Educational Institutions (Engineering Colleges) to Hospitals of repute and Engineering works to Hi-tech Software companies. Zandig have also provided CMMI & ISO project support to several Public Sector Banks in the country. Over a period of time the company has grown in size and today Zandig functions from BANGALORE/COIMBATORE.

We are one of the few NABET/QCI, New Delhi accredited (IRCA UK) recognized training providers in this part of the country for QMS/EMS/ISMS/OHSAS LEAD AUDITORS TRAINING PROGRAM. We have conducted over 400 LAT programs during the last 11 years in India and abroad.

The company’s QUALITY POLICY provides stress on total customer satisfaction by developing and implementing Quality Management System that meets the industry requirements. The training modules of Zandig Knowledge Management Pvt Ltd are such that they meet the state of the art Industry needs. These are ensured by continual improvement of the products and services. The mission of Zandig is to contribute towards prosperity to all stakeholders and the people at Zandig are committed to total customer satisfaction.

The Quality Management System and Environment Management System & OHSAS related trainings of Zandig have attracted a large number of participants and organizations. It is to the credit of Zandig’s faculties that so far over 1500 training programs have been conducted on quality related issues, such as QMS/EMS Awareness Programs, Internal Quality Auditor Trainings, Lead Auditor Trainings for QMS/EMS, Statistical Quality Control, Six Sigma, 5S Good Housekeeping, FEMA, Problem Solving Process and several other programs.

Zandig’s prestigious list of clients includes organizations such as IBM, WIPRO, State Bank of Travancore, Arvind Hospitals, Lohitha Group, Infosys, ITC Limited, Titan Company Limited, Automotive Axles Pvt.Ltd, Ananya Shelters, CERATIZIT Round Tool Solutions Pvt.Ltd, Dresser, Eye Foundation, General Electric, Retina Institute, Tanishq, Titan Eye Plus, VBL Innovations Pvt.Ltd, V.V.Mineral, Indian Rare Earths Limited, Voltas , Andhra Bank Branches, Jindal Aluminum, PSG College of Technology, and Several Textile Mills of huge size, Madura Coats, Industrial Minerals, National Institute of Fashion Technology, over 12 Engineering Colleges and many other organisations. Our list of medical institution clients include hospital like Wokards Hospital (Training in QMS) Bangalore, Auro Lab Madurai (QMS and NABH implementation) Geetha Hospitals amongst others.
